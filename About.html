MY JOURNEY.

I love hair bows so much. I started making them since when I was 9 years old. I gave them as birthday gifts to my friends in school and church. I loved the way it made people cute and lovely . Later on, I developed to making other hair accesories like hairbands of all kinds and every other thing . So I decided to start my brand of hair accesories. I love to see girls and ladies beautifully dressed. People will say starting a business is not easy, but consistency is the key. I will love to see you looking beautiful with your hair accesories on everyday,so patronize me today.
